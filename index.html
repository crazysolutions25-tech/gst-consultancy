GST & Tax Sections and Rules

Key sections and provisions under GST and Income Tax laws in India.

Section 22 deals with mandatory GST registration based on turnover. Section 24 covers compulsory registration regardless of turnover (e.g., inter-state supply, e-commerce operators).

Section 10 allows eligible taxpayers to opt for a simplified tax scheme with lower rates and reduced compliance.

These sections govern filing of outward supplies, monthly/quarterly returns, annual returns, and reconciliation statements.

Section 16 defines eligibility and conditions for claiming Input Tax Credit under GST.

Section 139 mandates filing of income tax returns by individuals, firms, and companies within prescribed due dates.
