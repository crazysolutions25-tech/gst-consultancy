Why GST Compliance Is Important

Goods and Services Tax (GST) compliance is mandatory for eligible individuals and businesses in India. Proper GST compliance helps avoid penalties, interest, and legal issues.

  • Avoid heavy penalties and late fees
  • Build trust with customers and vendors
  • Claim eligible Input Tax Credit (ITC)
  • Required for loans and financial records

Important GST Rules

GST registration mandatory under Section 22 & 24
Monthly / quarterly return filing is compulsory
Late filing attracts penalty and interest
Valid invoices required to claim ITC
